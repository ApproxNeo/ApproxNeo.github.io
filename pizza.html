Fresh Cheesy Pizza

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, pineapple, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.[1] A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta.

Ingredients to make pizza

1.1 1/2 cups (355 ml) warm water (105°F-115°F)

2. 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) of active dry yeast

3. 3 3/4 cups (490 g) bread flour

4. 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

5. 2 teaspoons salt

6. 1 teaspoon sugar

7. Toppings of your choice

Time to make pizza of your choice!

Time to make the dough

1.Make and knead the pizza dough: Add the flour,active yeast, salt, sugar, and olive oil, and knead using hand or mixer

Continue preparing your dough

2. Let the dough rise for 1.5 hours in a warm place.Spread a thin layer of olive oil over the inside of a large bowl. Place the pizza dough in the bowl and turn it around.
3. Divide the dough in half. Place on parchment paper or a lightly floured dish and place, uncovered, in the freezer for 15 to 20 minutes.

Add your toppings of choice

5. Flatten dough into round
6. Brush dough top with olive oil
7. Add food toppings of choice and customise it to your likings!

Bake and serve

8. Bake pizza in the 475°F (245°C) oven, one at a time, until the crust is browned and the cheese is golden, about 10-15 minutes.
9. Once done,It's time to serve the pizza
