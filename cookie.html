chocolate chip cookie

recipe🧈

Ingredients:

  1. White sugar (50g)
  2. Brown sugar (60g)
  3. Melted unsalted butter, at room temperature (90g)
  4. Baking soda (5g)
  5. All purpose flour (100g)
  6. 1 Egg (50g)
  7. Vanilla Extract (4g)
  8. Salt (2.5g)
  9. Chopped dark chocolate (as much as you want :) around 60-70% Cacao )

Instructions:

  1. Break down the butter into smaller chunks, and add into a microwave safe bowl and microwave on low power for 30 seconds.
  2. Remove butter from microwave and stir, repeat step 1 and 2 until butter is melted.
  3. In another bowl, add in brown sugar, white sugar and salt and whisk to combine.
  4. Add melted butter and vanilla extract into brown sugar, white sugar and salt mixture, stir until a paste is formed. (When picking up your whisk, you should see ribbons of mixture)
  5. In another bowl add all purpose flour and baking soda, whisk to combine
  6. Add all purpose flour and baking soda mixture into wet mixture from step 4 and gently fold with a spatula (avoid using a whisk, as heavy mixing would result in cakey and tough cookies!!)
  7. Chop the dark chocolate bar into chunks. The more uneven the better! It improves the texture :)
  8. Gently fold the dark chocolate chunks into your batter.
  9. Try your best to not eat any raw batter (although delicious, you might get salmonella and die)
  10. Transfer all cookie dough into a container and rest in the fridge for at least an hour and up to 36 hours. (Resting cookie dough allows flavours to develop and they will taste much better. But if you can't resist, them off immediately will also yield great results.)
  11. Preheat oven to 180 celsius, and bake cookies on a baking tray lined with parchment paper for 12~15 minutes until edges are slightly browned and crispy
  12. Enjoy the cooled cookies with a nice cup of milk or coffee! :) (try one of our recipes too!)

crispy & chewy Chocolate Chip Cookie 🍪

Here's the only chocolate chip cookie recipe you will ever need!

Tips:

  • Use chopped chocolate chunks! 🍫
    Chocolate chips do not melt as well as chunks, chopping your own chocolate will ensure fudgy pockets of chocolate everytime!
  • Sprinkle rock salt after baking! 🧂
    Saltiness gives a perfect balance to the sweetness of cookies, sprinkle some onto freshly baked cookies to enhance the depth of flavour!
  • Mix up the filling! 🍬
    Using different kinds of chocolate (milk, dark, mint) can yield very different cookies, feel free to try and see which you like best!

common mistakes ☹️

Cookies are tough and cakey You are overmixing your dough!
Mixing your dough too much will cause the gluten to tense up, resulting in a tougher texture. Try mixing more gently and for a lesser amount of time.
Cookies are burnt Your oven temperature is too high!
Having too high of an oven temperature results in your cookies cooking too quickly. Try reducing the temperature by 5-10 degrees.
Cookies not spreading Use parchment paper!
Parchment paper is a non-stick material that allows the dough to spread as it melts, baking them on any other surface would prevent them from spreading.
Cookies too crisp for me Cookies have too much white sugar!
Increasing the amount of brown sugar and decreasing white sugar will make the cookies more chewy.
My cookies are too salty You are using too much salt!
Also check if your butter is unsalted and not salted.
